Historical Sites Around Dindigul

 

Dindigul Rock Fort

Dindigul Rock Fort, a historical fort measuring 280 ft in height, is situated near the main town of Dindigul. The fort was constructed by Naik king Muthu Krishnappa Naicker in 1605. It is 2.7 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-8AM–5:30PM

 

Abirami Amman Temple

Abirami Amman Temple is situated in the heart of the city and is famous for the Kolu that is kept during the Navratri festival. During this festival, the temple priests perform over 1 lac archanas to Goddess Abirami Amman. A procession during all Fridays of Adi month is held at this temple when the goddess is taken on a flower pallak. It is 400 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-6.00 AM to 12.00 PM 4.00 PM to 9.30 PM

 

Green Valley View

Offers views of the plains and the Vaigai River Dam at the South. It is 100 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

 

St. Antony's Church

St. Anthony's Church was built between 1982 and 1989 by Thomas Fernando. The church is known for a ceremony that starts on the last Friday of January and continues for 15 days. It is 7 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-6:00AM-9:30AM and 11:00AM-6:15PM

 

Teepu Sultan Emperor Fort

A historical place. Rock fort is standing without much maintenance. We need to buy food items before climbing to the top. At top, we can see and admire about the tall fort and inside, lots of cave like forts for weapon storage. Also, we can see some big iron weapons. I prefer this place is good to watch with school children. Drawback is lack of maintenance. No place to take rest at the mid of the hill. It is 3 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-8AM–5:30PM

 

Thadikombu Perumal Temple

Thadikombu Perumal Temple is located at a distance of 5 km from Dindigul on the route to Karur. Lord Alagar is the main deity of this temple, who is offered a special pooja for 12 days during the Chitrai month from April to May. It is 10.2 kilometers from dindigul.

Visiting Timings:-6.00AM to 12.00PM and 04.00PM to 08.00PM
