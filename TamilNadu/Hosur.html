Historical Sites Around Hosur

 

Chandira Choodeswarar Temple

The Chandira Choodeswarar Temple is situated on top of a hill at Hosur. It is situated in the Krishnagiri District of Tamil Nadu and was built by Thirupuvanamalla Barvatharaja Anthiyazhvar of the Hoysala dynasty in the year 1260. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva or Choodeswarar and Goddess Parvati or Goddess Maragadambal.

The temple is also mentioned as Bhadragiri Mahatmiyam in Brahmanda Purana. This religious place has many theerthams including Jambava Theertham, Vrishabha Theertham, Maragatha Sarovaram (Karutmadam), Pandava Theertham, Shivagangai and Hanuma Theertham.. In fact, the whole temple is made with stones and there is a beautifully painted Rajagopuram on top of the prime entrance. Besides, it also has many other shrines of Ganesha, Lord Muruga and Nayanmars along with many other saints. It is 3.1 kilometers from hosur.

Visiting Timings:-6am to 12:30pm and 4pm-8pm

 

Maraghatha Sarovar

Maragatha Sarovaram is a theertham (pond) located in the premises of the famous Chandira Choodeswarar Temple in Hosur. The temple and the Maragatha Sarovaram is situated atop a hill called Bhadrachalam and is one of the most prominent shrines of the region.This ancient temple, built in 1260 by Thirupuvanamalla Barvatharaja Anthiyazhvar of the Hoysala dynasty, has a unique Architecture. The main deities of the shrine are Lord Shiva, Goddess Parvati and Goddess Maragadambal.

The major attractions here, apart from the Maragatha Sarovaram, are several other theerthams like the Vrishabha Theertham, Pandava Theertham, Jambava Theertham, Shivagangai and Hanuman Theertham, which are considered holy by the devotees.Many more shrines dedicated to Lord Muruga, Ganesha and various saints can be found near the Maragatha Sarovaram. The theertham and the temple are open from dawn to dusk and devotees can enjoy the serene atmosphere here.

The tourists visiting the Maragatha Sarovaram can also head to the other attractions nearby like the Murugan Temple, Kelevarapalli Dam, Rajaji Memorial and Hogenakkal Falls located in the vicinity. Amudagondapally village near Hosur has some rose gardens, which exports various kinds of roses to all parts of the world.Maragatha Sarovaram is easily accessed from Hosur by auto rickshaw and taxis. It is 2 kilometers from hosur.

Visiting Timings:-6am to 12pm and 4pm-7pm
