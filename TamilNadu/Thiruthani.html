Historical Sites Around Thiruthani

 

Arakkonam

Arakkonam is a small town in the Vellore District of Tamil Nadu, which lies in proximity to Thiruthani. The town is famous for ancient temples like Thiruvalankadu Vadaaranyeswarar Temple and Sholingur Narasimha Swamy Hill Temple.The Kanchi Kamakoti Agastheeswarar Temple, Paranji Sundara Perumal Temple, Nemili Bala Abirami Temple, Muthumari Amman Temple and Arthanareeswarar Temple are also situated near Arakkonam.This town is located near Thakkolam village, having seven Lord Shiva temples. Among these, the Jalanatheeswarar Temple, Somanatheeswarar Temple, Thirumambayanathar Temple and Payandiamman Temples are prominent ones. It is 14.6 kilometers from thiruthani.

Visiting Timings:-5:45 am to 8:00 pm.

 

Sri Venkatachalapathy Temple

Sri Venkatachalapathy Temple is located in the vicinity of Sivakasi. Located on the banks of River Vaipparu in Sattur town, the temple is dedicated to Lord Sathurappan (Vishnu).A prominent feature of this temple is the nine different standing postures of Sri Devi and Bhudevi, the consorts of Lord Sathurappan. This 800 year old temple became popular after a belief that a blind king regained his sight by worshipping at this temple. It is 77.9 kilometers from thirurhani.

Visiting Timings:-6AM to 9PM

 

Thiruthani Murugan Temple

Thiruthani Murugan Temple is a famous and ancient temple dedicated to Lord Muruga or Subrahmanya is located on a hilltop at Thiruthani. Legends have it that Lord Muruga got married to Valli at this holy place. This temple attracts a large number of pilgrims every year from near and far off places.Devotees coming here go up a winding flight of 365 steps indicating the number of days in a year. There is also a road which reaches the temple complex at the summit. The architecture of the temple and its gopurams (towers) and the sculpture of statues are eye-catching. The gold jewellery and garland decoration of the main idol further adds to its grandeur. Next to the presiding deity, there is a shrine dedicated to Lord Ganesh as well. There are large water tanks in and around the temple.Publications, calendars, rosaries, images and pictures of the deity, souvenir mementoes and puja articles are available in the shops around the temple.Nallatur Arulmigu Veera Anjaneya temple, Arulmigu Arumugaswami temple, Narasimma swamy temple, Velanjeri mangaleswar Sivan temple are nearby famous temples.There are ample buses and vans available to the temple from Thiruthani base. Winter is the best time to visit Thiruthani Murugan Temple. It is 2.6 kilometers from thiruthani.

Visiting Timings:-6:00am - 9:00pm
